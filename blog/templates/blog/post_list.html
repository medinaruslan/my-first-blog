My first day at KBTU

In the life of each student there was such an exciting moment as the first day at the university! This is a truly important moment in everyone's life. I thought that on the first day at the university everything would change dramatically and I would become a business lady, I know it all sounds funny, because nothing really happened, although I have to admit that on the first day at the university I found many new friends and still communicate very well with them. And my first day began not on September 1, but at the end of August, which I was very surprised about. On the same day in the evening at the university venues there was a KBTU OPEN concert, where everyone sang and danced. In general, it was very fun.
